BongoCat

Whats BongoCat?

BongoCat is a completely open source Discord Bot built in Python . It's really similar to bots like Dank Memer and OwO.

What does it offer?

  • User-interaction commands
  • Minigames
  • Images and memes
  • Welcomer
and much more.

Commands

ping
Check the bot's latency.
invite
Invite the bot to your discord server.
userinfo
Check a user's profile details. Also download their avatar and banner ;)
spotify
Check a user's Spotify listening activity. This command only checks for the user's Discord activity, this means it won't work if the activity is not displayed.
welcome set
Select a channel where to send the welcome message.
welcome remove
Remove the channel where to send the welcome message. No welcome message will be shown.
welcome background
Update the background for the welcome message. Select from the options.
welcome update
Update the channel where to send the welcome message.
newaccount
Create a new account for our economy system and start playing :)
delaccount
Delete your economy system account. It's sad to see you go :(
balance
Check your (or a user's) balance.
deposit
Deposit coins from your wallet to your bank.
withdraw
Withdraw coins from your bank to your wallet.
beg
